About Us

Toon Vanagt - Managing Partner

Toon has been a startup entrepreneur since 1999. His latest ventures are data.be (structuring Belgian business information from official sources) and lex.be (full text search in millions of legal publications). Both these freemium businesses are build on unstructured data and heavily relying on open data. Toon is a strong believer of all things open, which he supports via the non-profit Open Knowledge Belgium. Toon holds a Commercial Engineering degree from Solvay Business School at the VUB and was awarded "ICT manager SME of the year 2002" by Datanews. Toon followed the Virtualization industry for over a decade as the publisher of Virtualization.com

Quinten Vanagt - Project manager

Quinten Vanagt is a Brussels-based creative strategist with an extensive digital background. A KASK alumnus holding a Bachelor's in Media Art and a Master's in Fine Arts, he seamlessly blends artistic vision with emerging technologies. By leveraging innovative tools such as Gen-AI, 3D animation, Virtual Reality, and game development, he crafts digital experiences. As co-founder of NICE ARTS, Quinten has successfully developed projects for Brussels-based cultural institutions and commercial clients: This unique combination of creative expertise, technical innovation, and agile project management makes him an ideal partner for organizations looking to optimize their product, marketing and technology strategies.
